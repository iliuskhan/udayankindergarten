About Us

Udayan Kindergarten is dedicated to providing a warm and stimulating environment for young learners. Our experienced teachers foster creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning through play-based and structured activities. Established in 1996 [Shifted to own building in November, 2022], we aim to build a strong foundation for your child's future.
